# LLM Wiki Workflow

## Summary

This wiki follows the LLM Wiki pattern: raw sources are preserved, while the LLM maintains a structured markdown wiki that compounds project knowledge over time.

## How To Use

When a new experiment, tool, or source is discussed:

1. Save stable links or source snippets under `raw/`.
2. Update relevant synthesized pages under `wiki/`.
3. Add or update cross-links.
4. Update `wiki/index.md`.
5. Append a dated entry to `wiki/log.md`.

## Why It Matters

This project is accumulating knowledge across multiple platforms and tools. The wiki prevents useful findings from being trapped only in chat history.
